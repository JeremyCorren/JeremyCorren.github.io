About

I've composed music for Dustin Seo &
Marika Suzuki, Mivos Quartet, TAK Ensemble,
and Joshua Modney & Erin Lesser.

In March, I improvised in Tyshawn Sorey's
Conduction ensemble for Vijay Iyer's
residency Relation. November saw the
release of my recording with Dre Hocevar's
Large Ensemble, featuring Sam Pluta.

With Joel Ross's band, I've played at the
Earshot Jazz Festival, the Jazz Gallery, and
Smalls Jazz Club. A new record with that
band is slated for a 2017 release.
