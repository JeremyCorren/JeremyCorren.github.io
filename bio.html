Jeremy Corren

About

In August, I composed a piano trio for Dustin Seo
and Marika Suzuki, and a little piece for Mivos
Quartet in July. In the past, I've worked with members
of Talea Ensemble, TAK Ensemble, and Wet Ink.

November saw the release of my recording with
Dre Hocevar's Large Ensemble, featuring Sam Pluta, on
Clean Feed. A new record with Joel Ross's quintet
is slated for a 2017 release.

Since September, I have been actively contributing
to the development of dn-m, an automated music
notation software for iOS tablet devices.

I study at Columbia University.
