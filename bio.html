Jeremy Corren

About

I recently composed a piano trio for Dustin Seo
and Marika Suzuki, and a little piece for Mivos
Quartet. In the past I've worked with members of
Talea Ensemble, TAK Ensemble, and Wet Ink.

In March, I improvised in Tyshawn Sorey's
Conduction ensemble for Vijay Iyer's residency
Relation. November saw the release of my
recording with Dre Hocevar's Large Ensemble,
featuring Sam Pluta. A new record with
Joel Ross's quintet is slated for a 2017 release.

Since September, I have been actively contributing
to the development of dn-m, an automated music
notation software for iOS tablet devices.

I study at Columbia University.
