Jeremy Corren

Solo Piano

Improvisations for solo piano, taped at Columbia University
between 5/1/17 - 5/3/17.

'Exit signs'

'After one day'

'Partita'

'Onett afternoon'

'Hungry stars'

A set of short, self-contained meditations, inspired by years
of listening to the Scriabin preludes, Bill Evans' Alone,
Schoenberg's Hanging Garden songs, Keith Jarrett in solo
concert and the Ligeti piano etudes (esp. the slow ones).
