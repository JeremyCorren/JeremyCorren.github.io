Jeremy Corren

About

Recent pieces include a piano trio for Dustin Seo
and Marika Suzuki, a string quartet for Mivos Quartet,
and chamber works for members of the Talea Ensemble,
TAK Ensemble, and Wet Ink Ensemble.

I recorded with Joel Ross in December 2016 and in
Dre Hocevar's large ensemble with Sam Pluta for
Clean Feed in January 2016.

In Fall 2016, I contributed to the development of
James Bean's dn-m application, an automated notation
software platform for iOS.
