Jeremy Corren

About

Recent compositions include a piece for Mivos Quartet, a duo for Josh Modney and Erin Lesser from Wet Ink, and a piano trio for Dustin Seo, Marika Suzuki, and myself.

I regularly play in Joel Ross's quintet and have collaborated with Jasper Dutz in duo since 2011. A recording with Dre Hocevar's large ensemble was released in November 2016 by Clean Feed.

Between September and December of that year, I contributed to the development of dn-m, an automated notation software for iOS authored by James Bean.
